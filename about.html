What is a Cat Cafe?

About Kitty Cafe

Kitty Cafe was founded in 2010. It is a place for you and your cat to be treated like royalty. Don’t have a cat? No worries! We have several in need of warm, loving homes. We have a relaxed café setting where you can spend quality time with a furry feline of your choosing and you might decide to make them a part of your furever family too! We offer free wifi, a variety of beverages, and delicious desserts to snack on while visiting us! Come check us out!
